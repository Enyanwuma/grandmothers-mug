The Story

A quiet Saturday night.
A cylinder. Hours disappeared.

Three years ago, someone was trapped by expensive software subscriptions. A friend said: "Just get Blender. It's free."

That night, they modeled their grandmother's old ceramic coffee mug — the chipped one with faded flowers from the 70s. No tutorial. Just observation.

This guide is that night, documented.
